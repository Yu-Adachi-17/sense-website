## Atelier: AI Website Builder

Atelier helps you turn an idea into a polished website from your phone.

Create a live site in minutes with AI-generated pages, clean layouts, and fast editing built for real-world businesses and personal brands.

![Atelier generated website](/images/atelierintroduction3.jpeg)

Whether you are launching a salon, restaurant, portfolio, booking page, or link page, Atelier makes it easy to go from prompt to publish.

### What You Can Do with Atelier

- Generate a complete website from a simple prompt
- Edit sections, text, and structure without coding
- Publish instantly to a live Atelier URL
- Build pages for portfolios, local businesses, creators, and more
- Update your site anytime from your phone

Atelier is designed for people who want a beautiful site without the usual complexity. No desktop workflow, no design tools, no setup headaches — just describe what you want and launch.
