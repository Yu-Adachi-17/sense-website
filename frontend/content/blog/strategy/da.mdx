# Strategy

> **Not just minutes — <strong>Strategy</strong> uses modern AI (ChatGPT-5.2, Gemini 2.5 Pro) to turn discussion into next steps.**

## Hvad den gør
- **Boost**: det der virker og skalering
- **Counter**: risici og afhjælpning
- **Top Issue**: kernen der skal løses
- **Thinking modes**: Logical / Critical / Lateral

## Sådan bruges (kort)
1 Generér referat
2 Tryk “Strategy”
3 Gennemse Boost / Counter / Top Issue + 3 modes
4 Indsæt i dit opgavesystem

## Eksempel (meget kort)
- Topic: “Solar Chill” (summer blend)
- Risk: 15% say “too sour”

**Boost:** pilot 10 stores; measure 7-day re-order  
**Counter:** add “mild sweet” option; A/B syrup level  
**Top Issue:** widen appeal without losing distinctiveness  
**Logical:** segmenter → KPI’er → A/B → ship vinder  
**Critical:** valider ud over hype  
**Lateral:** duft/room-mist-udgave
