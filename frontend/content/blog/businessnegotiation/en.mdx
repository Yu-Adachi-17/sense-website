Negotiation is bargaining. Strong minutes don’t just list decisions and to-dos—they preserve key exchanges verbatim when needed, trace how terms changed (deadline/price/scope/responsibility), and record reasons and risks next to each decision. That context speeds judgment and reduces rework. With Minutes.AI, make this the default.
